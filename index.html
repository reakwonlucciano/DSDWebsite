Frequently Asked Questions

HOW LONG DOES YOUR SERVICE TAKE?

We have a Standard turnaround time of 5-7 working days. We also have an Express turnaround time of 2-3 working days. Additional paint/repair work can extend your service to 10 working days, if needed sooner please contact us before placing your order.

WHAT OTHER SERVICES DO YOU OFFER?

Mid-sole painting. Full trainer re-paint. Leather bag repaint. Minor glue work. Full sole gluing.

HOW DO I RECEIVE MY SHOES BACK?

We return all sneakers using our FREE DPD delivery service. This service allows you to track your shoes to within the hour of when they will be delivered using both email/text.
